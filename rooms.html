Luxury Rooms Summary

Welcome to Couples Resort, a luxurious haven where every room surpasses 5 Diamond/Star standards, nestled in the enchanting Algonquin Park. Our diverse accommodations cater to every couple's dream. The Chateau Rooms offer Old European charm with private gazebos and lake views, while the Spa Villas provide indulgent water luxuries. Masters Rooms blend boutique opulence with lakeside grandeur, and Chalet Rooms deliver rustic elegance with serene lakefront vistas. For the adventurous, the Exotic Rooms feature African-inspired décor, and the Juniors Rooms harmonize natural elements with the beauty of Algonquin Park. Each room is a unique blend of luxury, romance, and comfort, designed to create an unforgettable experience for every couple.

Each room at Couples Resort is a testament to our commitment to luxury and romance, thoughtfully designed to create an unforgettable experience. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or seeking a tranquil escape, our resort offers the perfect backdrop for couples to reconnect and create cherished memories in a setting that exceeds the highest standards of luxury and comfort.

Room Styles

Chateaus

Sqft: 1400

The Chateau Room, blending Old European elegance with modern luxury, features a private screened-in gazebo, walkout balcony, and an outdoor hot tub overlooking the stunning Galeairy Lake and Algonquin Park forests. This unique retreat, ideal for all seasons, allows guests to gaze at the stars from their deck's hot tub. Inside, the room boasts a 16-foot vaulted ceiling and a grand two-sided stone fireplace connecting the bedroom and living room. Furnished by The Art Shoppe of Toronto, the room offers an experience of unparalleled comfort and style.
