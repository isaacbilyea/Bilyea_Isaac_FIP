Gourmet Dining Summary

Executive Chef, Ronnie Coppens with Chef, Evan tantalize with their Brigade of 7 Chef d’Parties. Menus are focused on freshness, seasonal availability and Ontario produced foods. Only the highest quality products enter the kitchen where the team then produces meals; (Fresh Fish offered Daily as one of the Chef’s Daily features), from their expansive experience, that will tantalize your taste buds!

All room prices include a daily 5-star, classic 5-course gourmet dinner in our European style dining room or as room service brought to you. Every 5-course gourmet dinner includes 1 amuse bouche, 2 Starters (either Appy, Soup or Salad), 1 main Entree Choice and 1 Dessert Choice. A classic style breakfast in-bed is also included.
